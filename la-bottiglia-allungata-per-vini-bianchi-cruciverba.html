La Bottiglia Allungata Per Vini Bianchi Cruciverba: An Overview of This Unique Wine Bottle Shape

11 April 2023 by Admin

Wine lovers may have noticed that not all wine bottles have the same shape. While most red wines are bottled in the traditional Bordeaux bottle shape, white wines may come in a bottle with a longer and thinner neck. This bottle shape is known as "la bottiglia allungata per vini bianchi" in Italian, which translates to "the elongated bottle for white wines". This unique shape has become popular among winemakers in certain regions of Italy, such as Friuli and Trentino, and has even become a topic in crossword puzzles. In this article, we will explore the history and characteristics of this distinctive wine bottle shape.

The elongated bottle shape for white wines is believed to have originated in the regions of Friuli and Trentino in Italy. In this area, white wines are typically made from grape varietals such as Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, and Chardonnay, which have delicate flavors and aromas that can be better preserved in a bottle with a longer neck. The narrow neck of the bottle also allows for better control of the pouring of the wine, which is especially important for white wines that are often served chilled.

The elongated bottle shape for white wines has become a popular choice for winemakers in other parts of Italy and around the world as well. In addition to its practical benefits, the unique shape of the bottle also sets it apart from other wine bottles and adds a touch of elegance to the presentation of the wine. Some wineries have even created custom bottle designs that combine the elongated shape with beautiful labels and packaging to further enhance the appeal of their wines.

It is worth noting that not all white wines are bottled in the elongated shape. Some winemakers prefer to use the traditional Bordeaux bottle shape for white wines as well, and the choice of bottle shape may differ depending on the grape varietal, winemaking style, and marketing strategy of the winery. However, for those who appreciate the unique qualities of white wines bottled in the elongated shape, la bottiglia allungata per vini bianchi is a true delight for the senses.

For example, some German Rieslings are bottled in a similar elongated shape, which is known as the "Mosel bottle". This bottle shape is named after the Mosel river, which flows through the wine region of Mosel in Germany and is famous for its Riesling wines.

The Mosel bottle has a narrower neck than the Italian elongated bottle shape, which is said to enhance the floral and mineral notes of the Riesling wines. The Mosel bottle is also typically green or brown in color, which helps to protect the wine from sunlight and oxidation.
